About Saros

Saros is an AI‑powered knowledge portal that brings your Confluence spaces and internal docs straight into Slack. Using retrieval‑augmented generation (RAG) and embeddings, Saros delivers instant, context‑aware answers where your team collaborates.

  2. Google Drive (coming soon)
  3. Microsoft SharePoint (coming soon)
