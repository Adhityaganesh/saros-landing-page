Privacy Policy for Saros

Effective Date: April 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Saros ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Slack bot service that connects to Confluence workspaces.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Provided by Slack

  • Workspace ID and name
  • User ID and basic profile information of the installing user
  • Bot access tokens necessary for the functioning of our service

2.2 Information Provided by Atlassian Confluence

  • Cloud ID and workspace name
  • Access tokens to interact with your Confluence content
  • Information about Confluence pages and spaces you have granted access to

2.3 Content Data

  • Confluence page content for search indexing and embedding
  • Search queries you submit through the Slack interface
  • Interactions between users and the bot

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you and maintain your account
  • Provide the core functionalities of Saros
  • Process and respond to your search queries
  • Index and embed your Confluence content for searchability
  • Improve our services
  • Communicate with you about service-related matters
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely in our database hosted on Supabase. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, accidental loss, or damage.

Access tokens are encrypted and stored securely. We do not share your tokens or account credentials with third parties except as necessary to provide our services.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you wish to delete your account or request that we no longer use your information, please contact us at the email address provided below.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who help us deliver our services
  • Third parties if required by law
  • In connection with a business transfer (merger, acquisition)

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Restriction or objection to processing
  • Data portability

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

sarosknowledgebase@gmail.com

11. Compliance with Laws

We are committed to complying with all applicable laws and regulations, including GDPR where applicable.
